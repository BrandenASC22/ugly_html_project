Ben 10 Races

Let's learn about our Alien Races

Alien Races:

  • Alien X
  • Heat Blast
  • Ghost Freak

Alien X

Alien X. is a humanoid alien whose entire body is solid black, excluding his hands, which are white.

HeatBlast

HeatBlast . is a pyrokinetic, meaning he has the ability to generate and manipulate intense heat and fire from his body.

GhostFreak

GhostFreak. Ghostfreak is a phantom/ghost-like alien with a gaseous appearance and claw-like hands, and grey skin with black lines running all over him.
